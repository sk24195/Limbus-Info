What is Limbus Company?

Limbus Company is a story-based gacha game inspired by “Dante's Inferno”, made by Project Moon. You, the player play as Dante who manages the 12 characters through the journey to find the Golden Boughs. Each “Canto”, we explore every characters past and trauma.

Characters & Idenitiy

Limbus Company's characters are named “sinners”, each sinner being a reference to a literacy piece. Every sinner has at least 14 “identities”. Identities are the main part of the gacha system, each identities having unique abilities and skills.

For more information




E.G.O & Corrosions

An E.G.O is essentially your “ultimate” in a gacha game. These E.G.Os let you win clashes and support your team heavily throughout your journey. A corrosion is when an E.G.O is overused, or your sinner reaches a certain sanity threshold.
