Characters

In Limbus Company, each character is called a sinner, with every one of them referencing a literacy piece, such as Heathcliff from “Wuthering Heights”. Limbus Company explores each character's trauma and past each Canto, revealing each sinner's past and who they really are.

Identities

In Limbus Company, each identity is basically an alternate version of each sinner, with every one of them having different skills and abilities. These identities are the main thing you would be pulling for in the gacha. The rarity goes: 0, 00, 000. with one 0 being your base sinners, and 000 being strong.
